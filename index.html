About Us

Furry Friends Forever is a website that helps people look for dogs and cats specific to their requirements at local shelters in the Austin area. Our app helps take the extra stress off of physically going into a shelter and waiting hours hours on end just to walk around a sad smelly place. All you have to do is is go through a series of questions and at the end of the questionnaire a specific list of dogs or cats will pop up giving you pictures, descriptions, and contact info on where the animal is. This way you can contact the shelter and set up a meeting to meet your new furry friend!
